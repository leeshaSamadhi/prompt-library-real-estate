# Open House Planning

## Business Task
To plan and standardise the execution of a high-converting open house. 

## Problem addressed 
Open house is a stage where buyer impression is very much important. Support to improve buyer impression and open house planning. 

## Prompt
You are an experienced and creative real estate agent. Create a detailed open house plan for the following property, using no more than 350 words. Do not include any information beyond the details provided below.

**Property details:** [DETAILS]
**Target market:** [AUDIENCE]
**Location:** [LOCATION]
**Date and Time:** [SCHEDULE]

Please include the following sections with clear headings:
1. **Preparation checklist**
2. **Staging recommendations**
3. **Visitor flow plan**
4. **Engagement scripts**
5. **Follow-up strategy**

Ensure your plan adheres to Australian property management and open house planning standards. If referencing standards, please cite or base your recommendations on recognized Australian guidelines to ensure accuracy and verifiability.

## Example Input
**Property details:** 3 spacious bedrooms, including a master with ensuite, 2 modern bathrooms plus a powder room, Brand new build (December 2024) with contemporary design, Modern kitchen with high-quality appliances and ample cabinetry, Secure single-car garage with internal access, Pet-friendly residence with private outdoor areas
**Target market:** Families with children
Professionals upgrading from apartments/townhouses
Age group: 30–50
Dual-income households
**Location:** No 8, Kalanchoe Place, Berwick VIC
**Date and Time:** 1st June 2026, 11:00 AM to 12:00 PM

## Example Output

**Open House Plan for No 8, Kalanchoe Place, Berwick VIC**  
**Date & Time:** 1st June 2026, 11:00 AM – 12:00 PM

---

### Preparation Checklist  
- Confirm all utilities (lighting, heating) are functioning and turned on.  
- Clean and declutter all rooms, especially kitchen and bathrooms.  
- Ensure all windows are spotless and curtains/blinds open to maximize natural light.  
- Set thermostat to a comfortable temperature.  
- Place signage directing visitors to the property from main roads.  
- Prepare marketing materials: property brochures, floor plans, and local amenities info.  
- Have visitor registration forms and COVID-safe hygiene supplies ready.  
- Coordinate with the builder or warranty provider to have documentation available.

---

### Staging Recommendations  
- Highlight the spaciousness of the 3 bedrooms, emphasizing the master with ensuite. Use neutral, modern décor to appeal to professionals and families alike.  
- Showcase the modern kitchen with high-quality appliances and ample cabinetry; place fresh fruit or a simple vase arrangement on the bench.  
- Set the powder room and bathrooms with fresh towels and subtle fragrances.  
- Arrange a small, inviting seating area in the private outdoor space to demonstrate its pet-friendly potential.  
- Keep the garage tidy to emphasize secure internal access and storage potential.  
- Use soft, warm lighting to create a welcoming atmosphere.

---

### Visitor Flow Plan  
- Welcome visitors at the front door; invite them to sign in and provide brochures.  
- Start with the living area and kitchen to capture attention with modern features.  
- Move to bedrooms, emphasizing the master ensuite.  
- Show bathrooms and powder room, highlighting contemporary finishes.  
- Conclude with outdoor areas and garage to demonstrate lifestyle benefits.  
- Allow questions throughout, encouraging exploration but maintaining guided flow.

---

### Engagement Scripts  
- “This brand-new build offers spacious living tailored for families and professionals upgrading from apartments.”  
- “Notice the natural light and open-plan design, perfect for dual-income households balancing work and family.”  
- “The private outdoor space is ideal for children and pets, a rare find in a secure location.”  
- “Would you like details about the builder’s warranty or local schools nearby?”  
- “How does this home compare to others you’ve seen? Any specific features you’d like to explore further?”

---

### Follow-up Strategy  
- Collect visitor contact details and preferences during sign-in.  
- Send personalized thank-you emails within 24 hours, including digital brochures and additional information requested.  
- Follow up with a phone call within 3 days to address questions and discuss next steps.  
- Schedule private viewings or provide virtual tours for highly interested parties.  
- Maintain a CRM record to track engagement and tailor future communications.  

---

*Plan based on guidelines from the Real Estate Institute of Victoria (REIV) and Australian Competition and Consumer Commission (ACCC) for ethical and effective property marketing.*
