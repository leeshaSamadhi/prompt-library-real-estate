# Markerting Campaign Planning and Execution

## Business Task
Creates a detailed marketing campaign plan to position the property listing above competition while maximising the property visibility. 

## Problem addressed 
Reduce the cost of the long run marketing plan, saves times on manual campaign creation and supports to identify the hidden marketing efforts and stand on track with the realestate competitive market.

## Prompt

You are an expert marketing strategist and digital campaign manager at a real estate company. Please create a detailed marketing plan for the following property listing, strictly adhering to the specified constraints.

Property Details:

**Property Type:** [PROPERTY_TYPE]
**Location:** [LOCATION]
**Target Audience:** [TARGET_BUYERS/RENTERS]
**Property Features:** [DETAILS]
**Price/Listing Range:** [PRICE]
**Campaign Duration:** [DURATION]
**Campaign Budget:** [BUDGET]
**Special Considerations:** [CONSIDERATIONS]

Your marketing plan must include the following sections, each clearly labeled:

1. **Marketing Overview**
- Campaign objectives (e.g., quick rental, maximizing price)
- Target audience segmentation
- Key value propositions

2. **Channel Strategy**
- Online platforms (e.g., Realestate.com.au, social media)
- Offline marketing (e.g., leaflets, local ads, signboards)
- Justification for chosen channels

3. **Campaign Structure**
- Pre-launch activities (e.g., teasers)
- Launch phase (listing release)
- Ongoing promotion (ads, retargeting)
- Final push strategies

4. **Content Strateg**
- Create a sample social media post and provide additional content ideas
- Create sample email campaign content

5. **Lead Generation and Conversion**
- Lead capture methods
- Funnel design (awareness to offer)
- Follow-up methods

6. **Inspection and Engagement Plan**
- Open house promotion
- Buyer engagement tactics
- Personalization strategies

7. **Risk Assessment and Mitigation Plan**
- Market risks
- Campaign risks (e.g., low engagement)
- Mitigation strategies

Please provide your response in clear, concise bullet points or numbered lists. Avoid unsupported assumptions or unverifiable information. Where applicable, base recommendations on common real estate marketing practices or cite general industry standards. Limit your response to approximately 500-700 words.

## Example Input 

**Property Type:** Townhouse
**Location:** No 8, Kalanchoe Place, Berwick VIC
**Target Audience:** Families, young professionals, investors, renters
**Property Features:** 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms (including ensuite with master bedroom), modern kitchen, garage, powder room
**Campaign Duration:** 21 to 30 days
**Campaign Budget:** 10,000 AUD
**Special Considerations:** Emphasize the property’s value and benefits for better living.

## Example Output

**1. Marketing Overview**

- **Campaign Objectives:**  
  - Achieve a quick rental lease within 21-30 days  
  - Maximize rental price at $1500 AUD/week  
  - Generate interest from multiple segments for competitive offers

- **Target Audience Segmentation:**  
  - Families seeking space and comfort (3 bedrooms, ensuite)  
  - Young professionals valuing modern amenities and location  
  - Investors looking for stable rental yield in Berwick VIC  
  - Renters prioritizing convenience and lifestyle benefits

- **Key Value Propositions:**  
  - Spacious 3-bedroom townhouse with ensuite & powder room for family comfort  
  - Modern kitchen ideal for contemporary living and entertaining  
  - Secure garage parking adding convenience and safety  
  - Located in Berwick, a growing suburb with lifestyle and investment appeal  
  - Competitive rental price emphasizing value for money and better living


**2. Channel Strategy**

- **Online Platforms:**  
  - Realestate.com.au (primary listing platform) – industry standard for rentals in Australia  
  - Domain.com.au – secondary listing site to maximize reach  
  - Facebook and Instagram ads targeting segmented audiences (families, professionals, investors)  
  - LinkedIn ads targeting investors and young professionals  
  - Google Display Network retargeting visitors of listing pages

- **Offline Marketing:**  
  - Local leaflets distributed in Berwick and nearby family-centric neighborhoods  
  - Signboards at the property and nearby high-traffic locations  
  - Ads in local community newspapers and real estate magazines  

- **Justification:**  
  - Realestate.com.au and Domain are trusted platforms with high traffic for rentals  
  - Social media allows precise demographic targeting and retargeting, increasing engagement  
  - Offline channels reinforce brand presence locally and attract less tech-savvy prospects  
  - Signboards and leaflets boost local awareness and immediate inquiries


**3. Campaign Structure**

- **Pre-launch Activities (Days 1-3):**  
  - Teaser social media posts highlighting “Coming Soon” with key features  
  - Email blast to existing database announcing upcoming listing  
  - Set up landing page with inquiry form for early interest capture

- **Launch Phase (Days 4-10):**  
  - Official listing live on Realestate.com.au, Domain, and company website  
  - Launch targeted social media ads and Google Display campaigns  
  - Distribute leaflets and install signboards  
  - Announce open house dates

- **Ongoing Promotion (Days 11-25):**  
  - Weekly social media posts with new angles (e.g., family lifestyle, modern kitchen)  
  - Retarget ads to website visitors who didn’t convert  
  - Email follow-ups with new content and reminders of open house

- **Final Push Strategies (Days 26-30):**  
  - Countdown campaign emphasizing limited availability  
  - Special call-to-action: “Book your inspection now before it’s gone”  
  - Phone follow-ups with warm leads to close inquiries


**4. Content Strategy**

- **Sample Social Media Post:**  
  > 🏡 Just Listed in Berwick!  
  > Discover your perfect family home at No 8 Kalanchoe Place – 3 spacious bedrooms, modern kitchen, garage & ensuite!  
  > Rent for $1500/week – Live better, live comfortably.  
  > Book your inspection today!  
  > #BerwickLiving #TownhouseForRent #FamilyHome #ModernLiving

- **Additional Content Ideas:**  
  - Video walkthrough highlighting key features  
  - Carousel posts focusing on different rooms/benefits  
  - Testimonials or neighborhood highlights  
  - “Day in the life” post showing lifestyle benefits near the property

- **Sample Email Campaign Content:**  
  Subject: Your New Home Awaits at No 8 Kalanchoe Place, Berwick!  

  Dear [First Name],  

  We’re excited to introduce a stunning townhouse perfect for families, professionals, and investors. Featuring 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms including an ensuite, a modern kitchen, and secure garage – all at $1500/week.  

  Don’t miss out on this exceptional opportunity for better living in Berwick. Schedule your inspection today!  

  [Call to Action Button: Book Your Inspection]  

  Warm regards,  
  [Your Company Name]


**5. Lead Generation and Conversion**

- **Lead Capture Methods:**  
  - Inquiry forms on listing pages and landing page  
  - Social media lead ads with instant forms  
  - Phone call tracking from ads and offline channels

- **Funnel Design:**  
  - Awareness: Social media & online listings generate interest  
  - Consideration: Email nurture sequences & retargeting ads provide info  
  - Conversion: Open house and personal follow-ups encourage lease applications

- **Follow-up Methods:**  
  - Automated email confirmation upon inquiry  
  - Personalized phone calls to qualified leads within 24 hours  
  - SMS reminders before inspections  
  - Post-inspection thank-you and next steps communication


**6. Inspection and Engagement Plan**

- **Open House Promotion:**  
  - Promote dates via social media, emails, listing sites, and signboards  
  - Use RSVP system to manage attendance and create exclusivity

- **Buyer Engagement Tactics:**  
  - Provide property brochures with detailed features and benefits  
  - On-site agent to highlight value propositions and answer questions  
  - Virtual tour option for remote or busy prospects

- **Personalization Strategies:**  
  - Tailor communication based on audience segment (family, investor, etc.)  
  - Follow-up emails addressing specific needs or questions raised  
  - Use CRM data to customize offers or lease terms if applicable


**7. Risk Assessment and Mitigation Plan**

- **Market Risks:**  
  - Low rental demand due to market fluctuations or oversupply  
  - Competition from similar listings in Berwick

- **Campaign Risks:**  
  - Low engagement or poor lead quality  
  - Delays in listing approval or content production

- **Mitigation Strategies:**  
  - Monitor market weekly; adjust rental price or incentives if needed  
  - Increase ad spend on high-performing channels, pause underperformers  
  - Use professional photography and compelling content to stand out  
  - Early content creation and campaign setup to avoid launch delays  
  - Regular performance reviews and agile adjustments during campaign

- Contingency & Miscellaneous: $500
