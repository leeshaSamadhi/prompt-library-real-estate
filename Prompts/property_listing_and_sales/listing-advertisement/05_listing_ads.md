# Property listing and advertising 

## Business Task
Create the advertising plan after identifying the target market. 

## Problem addressed 
Reduces inconsistent listing quality and improve weak performance tracking

## Prompt
You are an expert digital marketer and branding specialist in real estate. Using only the information provided below, create a comprehensive property listing and advertising plan. Do not add, assume, or infer any details beyond what is explicitly stated. Your response must be no longer than 300 words.

**Propert details:** [DETAILS]
**Target market:** [AUDIENCE]
**Location:** [LOCATION]
**Key features:** [FEATURES]
**Special considerations:** [CONSIDERATIONS]

1. **Listing Overview:**
- Compelling headline
- Unique selling points

2. **Marketing Materials:**
- Photo/video recommendations
- social media campaigns
- Advertisement copy

3. **Offline Marketing:**
- Signage strategy 
- Print materials

4. **Lead Generation**
- Targeting strategy 
- Call to action design 

5. **Performance Metrics**
- Click through rates

6. **A/B Testing ideas**

Strictly use only the provided information. Avoid any assumptions, inferences, or added details. Use factual language only. Ensure your response does not exceed 300 words.

## Example Input
**Propert details:** 4-bedroom, 2-bathroom detached house with double garage, recently renovated kitchen, open-plan living, and landscaped backyard
**Target market:** Families, young professionals, investors, renters
**Location:** Berwick VIC
**Key features:** Close to schools and childcare, quiet residential street, large backyard, modern kitchen with stone benchtops, 5-minute drive to shopping centre
**Special considerations:** Highlight family-friendly lifestyle, safety, and proximity to schools. Emphasize move-in-ready condition.


## Example Output 
1. Listing Overview:  
**Headline:** Spacious 4-Bed Family Home with Modern Kitchen in Quiet Berwick Street  
**Unique Selling Points:** Recently renovated kitchen with stone benchtops; open-plan living; large landscaped backyard; double garage; close to schools, childcare, and shopping centre; move-in-ready condition.

2. Marketing Materials:  
**Photo/Video:** High-quality photos showcasing the modern kitchen, open-plan living, backyard, and double garage. A short walkthrough video emphasizing space and family-friendly features.  
**Social Media:** Targeted Facebook and Instagram ads focusing on families, young professionals, and investors in Berwick VIC. Carousel ads highlighting key features and neighborhood benefits.  
**Advertisement Copy:** “Discover your perfect family home in Berwick! This 4-bedroom, 2-bathroom gem features a modern kitchen, spacious living, and a large backyard on a quiet street, just minutes from schools and shopping.”

3. Offline Marketing:  
**Signage:** Professional “For Sale” sign at the property emphasizing “Family-Friendly - Move In Ready.” Include contact and viewing info.  
**Print Materials:** Flyers distributed locally and at nearby schools/childcare centers highlighting safety, schooling proximity, and lifestyle benefits.

4. Lead Generation:  
**Targeting:** Use location-based digital ads focusing on Berwick families, young professionals, and investors. Partner with local agents for direct outreach.  
**Call to Action:** “Book your private tour today and experience Berwick’s best family home—move in with confidence!”

5. Performance Metrics:  
**Click-Through Rates:** Monitor CTR on social ads aiming for 3-5%.  
**A/B Testing:** Test headlines (“Family Home” vs. “Modern Kitchen”) and CTA buttons (“Book Tour” vs. “Contact Agent”) to optimize engagement and inquiries.

## Automation Potential 
Moderate. Can be automated with CRM tools and with Ad platforms such as Meta and Google Ads. But a human invention is needed to take photos and videos. 

## Risks and Limitations
- Generic output production
- Lack of local market insights
- Compliance and legal risks - no misleading claims.
- Human execution required for photography.
